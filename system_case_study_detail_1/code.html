The Friction

Manual entry bottlenecks and data fragmentation between warehouse operations and accounting systems created significant overhead. Legacy workflows relied on manual exports and human verification of SKU movements.

The lack of real-time synchronization led to inventory discrepancies and delayed financial reporting, necessitating a unified automated architecture capable of handling multi-platform data flows without human intervention.
